Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I place an order?

Browse products, add them to your cart, and complete checkout with a payment method.

Q2: Can I cancel my order?

Yes, orders can be canceled within 24 hours via the "My Orders" section.

Q3: How do I track my order?

Go to the "My Orders" section to view your order status: Processing, Shipped, Delivered.

Q4: What payment methods are available?

We support UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery.

Q5: How do I request a return or refund?

Click on "Return" in your order details and follow the steps. Refunds are processed within 5–7 days.
